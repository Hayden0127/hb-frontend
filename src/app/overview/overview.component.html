Welcome to

The Beverly Hills Hotel

The Beverly Hills Hotel is a 5-star hotel with the persona of ‘home’. We welcome casual tourists and families as well as business travellers an unforgettable experience reminiscing the irreplaceable comfort of home, upped with attentive services and a panoramic view of the mesmerising South East China Sea. With 280 guestrooms ranging from premium rooms to simple rooms, all rooms are designed with your comfort in mind, the simplicity of the designs adds to the calmness of the surroundings.
